Don't know where to start? Here's all the information you need.

Post a Ride

Have extra seats on your next trip? Share your route and help someone get where they’re going.

How to Use:

  • Click “Post a Ride” in the navigation bar.
  • Fill in trip details: departure city, destination, time, date, and seats available.
  • Add any special notes like pickup point or preferences.
  • Submit and wait for ride requests from passengers.

Search for a Ride

Need a ride somewhere? Quickly find drivers headed your way.

How to Use:

  • Click “Search for a Ride” in the navigation bar.
  • Filter by departure city, destination, and date of trip.
  • Send a booking request to your preferred ride.

Manage Booking Requests

Keep track of who’s joining your ride and when the ride is complete.

How to Use:

  • Navigate to “Manage Booking Requests.”
  • As a driver, view and respond to incoming requests.
  • Once trip has been completed, the driver can mark the trip as completed.

View Trip History

Track your rides and stay organized.

How to Use:

  • Go to “Trip History.”
  • See your past rides as a driver or passenger.
  • Review trip details and participants.
  • Rate your experience with drivers or passengers.

Profile

Manage your account as well as reviews you've written and received.

How to Use:

  • Click “Profile” to update your info and picture.
  • View your reviews and trip ratings.
